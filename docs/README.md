# GGEM Farming Dashboard — Documentation

**Product name:** GGEM Farming Dashboard  
**Version:** 0.0.0 (pre-release ship candidate)  
**Last updated:** May 2026

This folder is the official documentation for shipping and operating the dashboard. It is written for **field operators and managers first**, with a **developer guide** for setup and maintenance.

---

## First release — what to expect

This dashboard is a **first release** (version **0.0.0**). It is live and ready for day-to-day use, but it is not the finished product.

**You may notice that some screens or menu items are still simple, or that a workflow does not yet do everything you would like.** That is normal for this stage. The team is actively building on what you see today.

**What is coming:**

- **More features** — manager tools, leadership views, reports, admin capabilities, and checklist improvements are being added and refined on a regular basis.
- **Regular updates** — the app will be updated as operations feedback comes in; documentation in this folder will be updated alongside releases when possible.
- **More departments and workflows** — today the focus is **Warehouse & Processing**, **Aggregation**, and **Data and Field**. Additional departments and checklists can be added over time as the business needs them.

If something you need is missing, tell your **manager** or **administrator** so it can be prioritised. Your feedback directly shapes what ships next.

## Support

For access issues (wrong role, locked out), contact your **administrator**.  
For wrong numbers in reports, verify the **submission was approved** and the correct **session ID** (aggregation) or **task** (warehouse) was used.
